## Venue

**Lancaster Castle**
Castle Hill, Lancaster LA1 1YJ


## Travel

Lancaster is well connected by rail on the West Coast Main Line (Avanti West Coast). Direct trains run from London Euston (~2h20mins), Manchester (~1h), and Glasgow & Edinburgh (~2h).

**Lancaster Castle is approximately a 5-minute walk from Lancaster railway station.**
